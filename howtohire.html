How to hire a bicycle

1. Step One

  1. Prepare your payment details
  2. Load mybike.com website
    • Click on any of the bike links to hire
    • You will be navigated to the hiring form

2. Step Two

  1. Start filling the hiring form
    • Fill in your first name
    • Fill in your last name
    • Fill in your email address
    • Fill in your phone number
    • Fill in the Start Date for the hire
    • Fill in the End Date for the hire
    • Fill in your visa/ debit card number
    • Fill in your CVV number
    • Fill in the card issued date
    • Fill in the card end date

3. Step Three

  1. Click the Hire Now button

4. Step Four

  1. Head over to your email address to confirm the received link with the confirmation receipt
